Sunday, April 7, 2024

It's finally starting to feel like spring and I'm starting to think about what I want to do this summer. One of the things I love to do is take art courses whenever possible. This summer I would love to go to Toronto and take a course at the AGO and to check out all of the amazing exhibits there. I was super excited to learn that AGO does have courses for Children and Youth . One of the special exhbits that I would love to see is the KAWS: Family exhibit .
